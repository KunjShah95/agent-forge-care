Free trial | Premium: $2.95/week

$150-300 per mock interview

Free

7. Simplify

Free tier | Pro: $9/month

Chrome extension that autofills job applications using your saved information. Speeds up high-volume applying.

Free (40 jobs) | Pro: $10/month
